Short Description of the Nazgul

These are the Nine Nazgul or ringwraiths who were originally the Nine Men the Nine Rings were bestowed upon. They are invisible to most creatures of Middle-Earth in the real world, but once the One Ring is put on your finger, you become invisible too and can see the ringwraiths (amongst other creatures) in the invisble world as well.

This image of a singular Black Rider is from the Lord of the Rings movie, simply showing off the dark character of the Nazgul.



This picture of multiple ringwraiths is from the Lord of the Rings movie as well, when the hobbits are being followed and charged on.



This drawing of all Nine of the Nazgul with their swords down on the ground is most likely a fan art creation that shows how cool they all really are.
