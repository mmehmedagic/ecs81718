My Biography (so far):

I was born in Chicago, Illinios, on July 13, 2004. I grew up in Chicago and lived in the same household for my entire life with my mom, my dad, my brother, and one of my grandmothers. My parents are from Bosnia, so we go to Europe often to see family. In addition to that, I speak both the languages of Bosnian and English. I started school when I was 4 years old in Waters Elementary, and I stayed in Waters for 8 years up until 6th grade. I then transferred to Lane Tech Academic Center and began to venture into a new part of my life. I have stayed in Lane up until my 8th grade year now in 2018 and plan to stay here for the rest of high school. Throughout this 13-year life of mine, I have mostly watched TV, played videogames, and ate a lot of food.
