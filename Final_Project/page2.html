Schooling

I started school when I was four years old in Pre-K level at Thomas J. Waters Elementary School. I got A's (for the most part, some B's, :P) in all of my subjects. My favorite subject was most definitely math, and in 4th grade, I decided to put extra effort into learning more math. In 6th grade, the administartion of Waters put me in a higher level (I didn't ask for it), so Ive been highly accomplished/ahead ever since. Living in the same house in Chicago my entire life, I had the ability to never transfer schools until high school. However, in 6th grade, I tried out for Lane Tech Academic Center and made it in, so I accepted, packed up all my bags, and said goodbye to all my friends (until I saw them after school every day next year). I've been in Lane Tech ever since and yet I am still ahead in math weirdly enough. I plan on staying here for the rest of high school, unless something major happens.
